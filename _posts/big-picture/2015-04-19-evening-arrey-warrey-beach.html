The time had come to do a road trip. Given the responsibility of transporting my sister's car from Mumbai to Goa, Priya & I decided to drive along the west coast, mostly on NH4 Maharashtra State Highway 4 and taking roads along the sea side whenever possible. We broke the journey over three days, and on day two we found ourselves at the Arrey Warrey beach during sunset.

#Priya (@happyfeetus) and I are on a #road #trip from #Mumbai to #Goa. We've hijacked Jolene's (@makewayforjo) car, ditched the celebrated highway and hit the #coastal route on a whim. We've have come across some of the most pristine #beaches in India, which easily beat the over commercialized beaches of #Goa. We've seen some breathtaking vistas of the coastal konkan ghats and driven through beautiful, idyllic villages. We've met some of the nicest people (only in the villages though) and driven through some of the best/worst roads. Today is the last leg of our 3 day trip. If you see us in a red MH car in Goa, be nice. #drive #coast #beaches #roads #travel
