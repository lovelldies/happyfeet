Bara Gumbad, the 520-year old, three-domed mosque located inside the Lodhi Gardens in Delhi, speaks tomes of the elegance of Islamic architecture with its intricate Arabic calligraphy arranged in beautiful engravings and exquisite floral and geometric patterns.

On a beautiful spring afternoon, it was a delight to explore this structure and walk around the lovely Lodi Gardens.
