Munnar was filthy. The town was just loaded with dust, tourists who kept littering and speakers blaring out noise, trying to keep all the gods from sleeping. We decided to stay in Devikulam, a small town some 8 kilometers away. The next morning we headed to Top Station, passing under, through and above clouds as we made our way there.

40 odd kilometers later we were there. Here are a few photos clicked during our ride.
