I’m no early bird. It is very rare that I’m watching the sun rise. But thanks to the Champions League final, followed by some non stop FIFA, we (Daniel & I) were up till dawn and finally decided to call it quits and head home.

{% include image.html loc = 'https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/rzOvNHDICkKGEGUKb-dtbhmr-ijCpVIWVeKEx8FH_yqf=w1600' alt = 'Driving along the Ribandar causeway.' %}

I had to make my way from Tonca (Panjim) to Old Goa. The road home is a drive along the Mandovi River. Here is an old video of me driving around Goa, which will give you an idea of what the drive is like (although I’m driving in the opposite direction in the video).

{% include image.html loc = 'https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/zZoYjRpIBUxA_Ql3fZNux4BRVnG6r9PPdIyzyS45C0tu=w1600' alt = 'Break of dawn.' %}

I made a pit stop opposite the Goa Institute of Management and took a few photos, while I enjoyed the sunrise.

{% include image.html loc = 'https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/SfKOj0Q10e8iUR-U4BaywueJEx2IgYtmX_ngCeoRQsfh=w1600' alt = 'Sunrise at Ribandar.' %} {% include image.html loc = 'https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/ry7h46UUmq8PbHe7zWVxf-A2Ng9tzmev_4JGLFexEjkT=w1600' alt = 'Hello World! — The sun pops out.' %} {% include image.html loc = 'https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/wQjjqKun6Mh1daCKiuMrEgEa9msDCPe1ZuhLlfmkrKnJ=w1600' alt = 'Reflections on the calm Mandovi river.' %}

And finally I was home! Where the clouds continued to put on a show.
