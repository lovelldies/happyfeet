Faith and frenzy go hand in hand in a country like ours. We, as people, feel the need to believe. In a higher power. In a God that is often cruel, but has a ‘plan’.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_e02449d4b7_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8030/8038576586_79d656c029_h.jpg 1600w" %}

That’s what we keep saying to ourselves. There is a plan for us. A role in a grander scheme of things. And we have to believe this illusion because not believing it is scarier. Not believing it makes all the bad things around us seem pointless. Like everything is happening for no reason and is actually nothing but a sorry accident. And that idea of truth terrifies us. So we believe. With fervour. With blind faith. With a madness that is always unexplainable, but sometimes understandable.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_dc65aa21a2_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038576772_b776539d23_h.jpg 1600w" %}

We are a sea of people. Hungry, not for happiness, but approval.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_7e422dc9ca_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8179/8038572897_09b086b1ef_h.jpg 1600w" %}

Approval from that one divine presence. One that we have to please at all costs.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_5d94a8f790_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038573071_2cd92a217d_h.jpg 1600w" %}

So we do everything it takes to appease this entity. This magical creature. Who is an apparition to some. A daydream to others, but an idol to us.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_7ea032bb4b_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8169/8038573231_0da8446b49_h.jpg 1600w" %}

He sits on our heads and watches our every move so that we don’t err.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_21bd741a8c_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8310/8038577450_9363603ec3_h.jpg 1600w" %}

But err, we do. We err and pray for instant forgiveness. We hold our ears and break coconuts. And then we err some more.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_dc5ef231cd_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8316/8038573651_a93a865c27_h.jpg 1600w" %}

We wait patiently for our prayers to come true. We watch and do nothing as everything around us burns, self-destructs and disappears. But we keep waiting. For miracles. Every single day.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_a4df83e419_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8176/8038577814_e62aca924a_h.jpg 1600w" %}

And when we look back, we don’t see what we lost when we were busy being blinded. But we see stories. Of how our faith actually changed our lives and made us better people.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_a44fb09b19_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8035/8038577944_b5fc16aa03_h.jpg 1600w" %}

And we truly believe those stories.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_3bceee21b3_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8042/8038574101_2c3e2c0599_h.jpg 1600w" %}

Because it’s these stories of hope and the promise of redemption that make us want to keep hanging on.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_7a20bca778_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8032/8038574253_da8784c8fa_h.jpg 1600w" %}

Because believing in these stories is easier than believing in ourselves.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_dbdb1ddb4f_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8459/8038578578_092586526d_h.jpg 1600w" %}

After all, we say, we are mere mortals. Helpless at the hand of fate and the mercy of divine power. Faceless devotees of that Omnipresent being.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_26b8cddd90_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8455/8038574803_599cab56e7_h.jpg 1600w" %}

We are actually just floating along our lives powerlessly like a piece of debris. With no control over anything. With no free will.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_bef855f068_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8460/8038578948_370eca0a67_h.jpg 1600w" %}

But we don’t know this yet. Because in our subconscious battle between faith and free will, it’s faith that wins.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_ff5edd9382_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8181/8038575197_b8f966a9c0_h.jpg 1600w" %}

But maybe, that isn't so bad. Maybe this kind of faith is not all that blind and baffling. While some believe in magic, love and happiness, others believe in God.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_ddb57ab852_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8170/8038575329_07bde613cf_h.jpg 1600w" %}

But either way, at the end of it all, we are all just hanging on to abstract ideas.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e684d67838_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8315/8038579388_e63b16c269_h.jpg 1600w" %}

So yes, the truth and the reality may be two different things for us. But till the day we keep believing, we can live unafraid. Reassured.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_7bccd16f20_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8177/8038579534_be83d29169_h.jpg 1600w" %}

We live knowing that our actions – our very mortal deeds and human failings – will be evaluated fairly someday.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_c358981c81_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8457/8038579736_841b7cf4db_h.jpg 1600w" %}

Not evaluated anytime in this life maybe, but in the afterlife by an all-seeing God who will divide the rights from the wrongs. The goods from the bads. No matter how many masks we wear in our lifetime, we believe, he will see right through us.

{% include image.html loc="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90_n.jpg" alt="Ganesh Chaturthi, Hyderabad (2012)" datasrcset="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90_n.jpg 320w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90.jpg 500w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90_z.jpg 640w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90_c.jpg 800w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_7454705f90_b.jpg 1024w, https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8456/8038575895_2ade3573cc_h.jpg 1600w" %}

So, we hang on dearly to the idea of him.
